Support

Help for learning, accounts, and app issues

Contact support

Open an issue in the Lingua Loop GitHub support tracker. Please do not include passwords, access tokens, private learning data, or other sensitive information.

What to include

Describe the screen, the steps that caused the issue, your platform, and the app version. For a vocabulary report, use the in-app report action so the word identifier and context can be attached without exposing private account data.

Common fixes

  • Restart the app and check that notifications or microphone access are allowed when the feature needs them.
  • For sync problems, reconnect to the internet, keep the app open briefly, and retry. Local progress remains available offline.
  • For audio or translation problems, retry when online; these optional services do not block learning.

Support availability and response time may vary. The publisher should confirm the final support process before store submission.
