Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 July 2026

This policy explains how Lingua Loop handles information when you use the application.

Information stored

Learning progress, settings, vocabulary activity, saved words, collections, and review history may be stored locally on your device. If account sync is enabled, the same learning data and profile settings may be stored in the Lingua Loop Supabase project so they can be restored across signed-in devices.

Account information

If you create an account or connect an identity provider, the authentication provider may provide an email address, provider identifier, and basic profile information. Lingua Loop uses this information to authenticate you and associate your learning data with your account.

Permissions and notifications

Notifications are optional and require device permission. The app stores your notification preferences and schedules reminders according to those preferences. Microphone access is not required for the current vocabulary learning features.

Service providers

Optional services such as authentication, synchronization, translation, audio, and crash or analytics providers may process the minimum data needed for the enabled feature. Vocabulary text is not included in analytics event names or properties.

Retention and deletion

You can reset local learning data from Settings. If account sync is enabled, contact support to request account data deletion. Removing the app may not delete data already stored by an external authentication or sync provider.

Children

Lingua Loop is an education product and is not knowingly directed at children under the minimum age required by applicable law. Do not create an account if you are not permitted to do so in your jurisdiction.

Contact

For privacy or support requests, use the Lingua Loop support page.

This page is a product privacy notice and should be reviewed by the publisher for the launch jurisdictions before store submission.
